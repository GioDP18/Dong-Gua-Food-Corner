About Us

Dong Gua Food Corner

Dong Gua Food Corner is a startup business located at Eastern Visayas State University_ Carigara Campus, specializing in products made from winter melon, also known as dong gua. The business offers a variety of snacks and beverages using winter melon as the main ingredient. With a focus on fresh and locally sourced ingredients, Dong gua food business aims to provide delicious and nutritious options for students and the surrounding community. Their unique concept and commitment to quality make them a promising addition to the local food scene.

FAQs

What is Dong Gua Food Corner?

Dong Gua Food Corner is a food business that specializes in dishes made from winter melon, also known as dong gua.

What are the payment methods accepted at Dong Gua Food Corner?

We accept cash payments as well as GCash transactions.

Are the products at Dong Gua Food Corner healthy?

Yes, our products are made from winter melon which is a nutritious and low-calorie ingredient. We prioritize using fresh and high-quality ingredients in our dishes.

What are the operating hours of Dong Gua Food Corner?

We are open from Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM for your convenience.

Do you offer delivery services?

Currently, we do not offer delivery service outside of the campus where we are located, but we do offer delivery service within the campus.
